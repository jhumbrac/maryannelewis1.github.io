Birdsong Stationery offers everything you need for writing to anyone and everyone. We create a special message that you can craft as you like, sending a card or a letter with a unique touch.

If you're looking for cards or stationery:

  • We can help if you're planning a wedding
  • If you're interested in graduation invitations and thank-yous
  • If you want something really unusual
  • If you've tried other card companies and just weren't satisfied.

Good cards for you!

Plain old cards for any occasion can be purchased, from Happy Birthday to Happy New Year. Birdsong Stationery is your local cardshop.

Something to write with!

We not only offer stationery, we sell pencils, pens and markers! In a variety of colors, our pencils, pens and markers are memorable, whether it's a rainbow set of markers, or a deep blue or completely black pen.

Bridesmaids Thank-You Notes

Special events are made for us. Are you looking for supercreative birthday invitations? We have them. What about a gift note for your bridesmaids? If you can come up with an idea, we can come up with the note.

Monograms!

And what about personalized stationery? We can create monogrammed envelopes or print addresses on writing paper or create a notepad with any message you'd like on it.
